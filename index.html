Hi! This is the portfolio of Niels Uiterwijk, a game developer located in The Netherlands. Currently working at Codeglue on various projects such as Antegods, Rocket Riot and Terraria. In my free time I participate in game jams, create my own little games or work on side projects such as my multithreaded renderer. Here you can view my resume or if you want to contact me: nielsuiterwijk@gmail.com

Professional Projects

Antegods

Antegods is an action-packed arena game in which the remnants of the Mayan civilization have taken to space.

Terraria

Dig, fight, explore, build! Nothing is impossible in this action-packed adventure game.

Rocket Riot 3D

Get ready for Rocket Riot, an action-packed, side-scrolling, 8-bit shooter where the rockets and the fun never stops!

ibb & obb

ibb & obb is a two player cooperative game set in a puzzle filled world where gravity goes both up and down.

Awesomenauts

Awesomenauts is a 2D MOBA game where two teams of three space mercenaries try to destroy each others' base.

Swords & Soldiers

Rewrite history as you take control of the brutal Vikings, the devious Aztecs or the crafty Chinese in their global quest to win the favour of the Gods!

Last Inua

A captivating experience, Last Inua tells the tale of an Inuit family’s fight for survival in the harsh elements of the mythical Arctic.

Body & Brain: The Pharao’s call

Take control of a young teenager traveling through time, solving puzzles with knowledge about the past, and find your way back home!
